Welcome!

We would love to welcome you to our cosy separated vacation home in Thalheim. Get exited for calm place with your own roofed patio on your own yard with a barbeque area as well as a sunbathing area. And free Wifi is also available of course.

The vacation home ist the ideal place to do some daytrips to the near Donautal which is 5km (3 miles) away, to the Seepark in Pfullendorf (23km - 14 miles) or to the Lake of Constance (35km - 22 miles). The nearest stores are located in Messkirch which is about 7km (4.5 miles) away from the vacation home.

Our Vacation Home

The separated and with love furnished vacation home of about 45 qm (484 sqft) can offer you a bedroom with a queensized bed and a sink, a small kitchen, a bathroom with a shower and toilette, a living- and diningarea as well as a loft which can sleep abot two more people. The kitchen is stocked with most neccesaties as well as a coffe maschine, a toaster, a water boiler and dish towels. The dining table can sit up to four people and offers enough space to comfortably eat and spend the evening with some nice company. The fireplace in the livingroom makes even cold evenings enjoyably warm and the comfortable chairs invite you to snuggle up and enjoy the warmth. A Hi-Fi unit and TV are supplied as well. The vacation home is most suitable for up to 4 people.

The yard with a lawn for sunbathing, a barbeque area and a roofed patio is a great spot to relax and enjoy your day.
